Alumni Association

The American College Alumni Cell (ACAC) has been a nodal forum for its alumni to initiate dialogue among themselves and to provide constructive direction to further the mission of the College in the field of higher education. As of now, the American College Alumni Cell has a collective of social scientists, scientists, civil servants, literary critics, media experts, journalists, political leaders, social activists, technologists, managers and entrepreneurs. It recognizes its alumni as important stakeholders in its continuing mission to provide excellent education.


The American College Alumni Cell has involves in digitalizing the profile of all the alumni to create virtual database so as to facilitate the fellowship among alumni and between alumni and the College. We seek your support to carry forward the mission of the College.


The Alumni Cell office of the American College is also committed to establishing links between various prospective employers and the American College students through their departments. Any alumnus can get included in this directory by sending us details at acalumnicell@gmail.com
